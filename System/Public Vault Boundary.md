# Public Vault Boundary

This vault is a technical learning display layer.

## Allowed

- Mathematics notes
- Computer Science notes
- Communications and wireless channel notes
- Cleaned concepts, maps, formulas, algorithms, and diagrams

## Not Allowed

- Humanities, history, politics, and social science reading notes
- Private reflections or unresolved comments
- WeRead raw exports or draft extraction files
- Long copyrighted excerpts
- Links that only make sense inside the private vault

## Source

Cleaned notes should be exported from the private vault only after they are reviewed there.
